# Usage Guide

## Step 1: Input Syllabi

Paste or upload one or multiple course syllabi.

## Step 2: Generate Plan

Click "Generate Plan".

## Step 3: Review Output

The system will display:

- Weekly prioritized to-do lists
- Study guide recommendations
- Explanation of task prioritization

---

## Notes

- The AI analyzes workload distribution.
- Tasks are ordered based on deadlines and grading weight.
- Future versions will support Canvas integration.
